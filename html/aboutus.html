Introduction

Started with someone who wishes to overcome his fears, Remember The Name wishes to expand this experience through Excursions here in Azores.

We think it's important for you to face your fears, and why not do so in such a beautiful green place, through radical sports.

In order to grow as a person, one must step outside the comfort zone, no matter what that may be. Here in COMPANY NAME we hope to provide you the challenging experience you'd certainly opt out of.

Our origins

We're a multicultural group based in Terceira, the land of dairy. Our favorite part about Terceira is that we're an island isolated by the sea from the current pandemic. There's a kind crowd here that keeps this community warm and welcoming

You may find us lurking at a local residency or, laptop in hand, in little cafe.
